    PERFECT FOR FAMILIES AND GROUP STAYS

    Three double bedrooms can comfortably accommodate parties of up to six. Plus, the large living room, dining room and kitchen offer space to host a dinner party, hold a meeting or celebrate with friends.

    Ryde House has a beautiful walled courtyard garden perfect for alfresco dining.

    Ryde House is also popular for wedding parties, with its proximity to the three churches in town, off street parking, and lovely garden.

    IN THE AREA

    From award-winning delicatessens to local butchers and the finest wine merchants, everything you need for an exemplary dinner party is right on your doorstep.

    Petworth is also a perfect location for antique and gift shopping at local boutiques, visiting art galleries and country walks. Once you have explored Petworth House and the town’s other main attractions, there is so much more on our doorstep including the South Downs National Park, vineyards and some of the UK’s best known sporting attractions at Goodwood and Cowdray.
